5 Minute Breakfast Burritos

I prepare these burritos for the week ahead if I'm on my A-game, but they're quick enough to throw together at a moment's notice before work.

Ingredients

  • Mission flour tortillas (burrito size)
  • Par-cooked hashbrowns or potatoes o'Brien
  • Costco Mexican cheese blend
  • Pre-cooked breakfast sausages
  • Your favorite salsa

Instructions

  1. Place burrito flat on a plate.
  2. Add 1/4 cup of mexican cheese.
  3. Add quarter cup of potatoes.
  4. add 2 sausages in a row along the length of the burrito.
  5. drizzel a spoonful of salsa evenly over the contents of the burrito
  6. Fold the burrito, being sure to tuck in the ends
  7. (Optional) wrap burrito in a paper towel and pack in a sandwich bag, refrigerating until ready to serve
  8. microwave for 90 seconds to serve
