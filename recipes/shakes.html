Chocolate Banana Protein Shakes

These protein shakes were made for gaining muscle during a bulk cycle, and use a generous amount of peanut butter to add calories (and protein). Those of you in a cut cycle have been warned!

Ingredients

  • Oat milk (or your preference of dairy/dairy-alternative)
  • Protein Powder
  • Peanut-butter (I recommend against shelf-stable varieties for this recipe)
  • One Banana
  • Cacao Powder

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend thoroughly
  3. It's just a shake, what do you want from me?
  4. Drink it yo
