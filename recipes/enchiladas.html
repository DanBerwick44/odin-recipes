Rotisserie Chicken Enchiladas

These enchiladas make a great meal prep! They're cheap, they're relatively quick to assemble, and they reheat super well!

Ingredients

  • Costco Rotisserie Chicken
  • Storebought corn tortillas
  • Costco Mexican cheese blend
  • Jarred Salsa Verde
  • Cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350
  2. Carve rotisserie chicken and coarsely chop or shred.
  3. Taking a tortilla in hand, sprinkle about 10g of cheese into tortilla and spoon a 1/4 cup of shredded chicken on top.
  4. Wrap the tortilla and flip it into a baking dish so that the joined ends are facing down.
  5. repeat steps 2-4, starting from the edge and using the previous enchilada to support the next.
  6. One the baking dish is filled, sprinkle with cheese. Broil in the oven for about 5 minutes or until cheese just begins browning.
  7. Remove baking dish from oven and turn off broiler, keeping the oven at 350 degrees.
  8. Smother enchiladas in salsa verde and return to oven for 20 minutes.
  9. Remove enchiladas from oven and garnish with cilantro.
